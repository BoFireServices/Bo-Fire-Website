About Us

BO Fire is a fire safety company based in Pretoria, operating since 2011. We offer fire extinguisher servicing and refills, servicing of all fire equipment (hose reels, hydrants, alarms, etc.), new fire equipment sales, compliance certificates, and fire safety training. We are BSI & SAQCC compliant, with registered and experienced technicians. We serve Pretoria and surrounding areas and can be contacted at 083 294 1917 / 012 004 1437. We are located at 68 Annie Botha Avenue, Riviera, Pretoria, 0084.
